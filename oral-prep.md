# CSS - What and Why: 
- background-image : url('image url'): 
    is used to add a image as a bg
- background-size : cover;
    is used to make the background cover the div
- background-postion: center;
    to center the background image
- padding: 
    to give content space around it inside the container
- margin: 
    to give space of the conmtainer outside
- font size :  
    to give font a size
- color: 
    color of the text
- font-weight bold:
    to make font bold
- border-radius: 5px: 
    to edit on the border raduis
- background-color: transparent: 
    to make the background transparent

## flex box: 
- display: flex
    to give the propirty of flex boxes
- flex-direction: column: 
    by default its row , can be changed to column
- flex-wrap: wrap: 
    by default no wrap, to make things wrap
- justify-content: center , start , end ,       space-around , space-between , sapce-evenly: 
    aligns on the x axis
- align-items: center , start , end , stretch: 
    aligns itmes on y axis
